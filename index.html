Designed for all types of players
Casual
You enjoy playing games for the story and the experience. You're looking for a game that introduces new mechanics slowly and does not require precise technical input to complete the story.
Kingdom of Light gradually and safely introduces players to new mechanics and enemies before ramping up the difficulty.
Kingdom of Light rewards exploration with lowering the difficulty of the game. If the player gets stuck moving in one direction there’s many other paths the player can explore. Then they can return to the previously too difficult area powered up and clear it easier with more health, greater damage, or new movement techniques. Exploration and killing enemies rewards you with money which can be used to purchase movement techniques at the town which will make moving around more fun and dodging enemies or advancing easier
Kingdom of Light offers multiple paths to the same goal, but guides the player with an invisible hand preventing backtracking to too many areas that the player should not have access to in order to reduce frustration.
The in game map hints to important locations and keeps track of where the player has been so in subsequent play sessions they can pick up where the left off
