Flying Fire Presents:
Easy to Pick Up, Challening to Master
Casual
You enjoy playing games for the story and the experience.

You're looking for a game that introduces new mechanics slowly and does not require precise technical input to complete the story.
Venture through Light and Darkness
Learn new mechanics in the light, and put them to the test in the darkness.
Power Ups Around Every Corner
Rewards exploration with lowering the difficulty of the game.

If you get stuck going in one direction there’s many other paths to explore.

Exploration and killing enemies rewards you with money for purchasing movement techniques at the town which will make moving around more fun, dodging enemies easier, and allow for clearing new obstacles.
Explore an Interconnected World
Multiple paths to the same goal.

Guides the player with an invisible hand by preventing backtracking to too many areas at a time.
Detailed In-Game Map
The in game map hints to important locations and keeps track of where the player has been so in subsequent play sessions they can pick up where they left off.
Speed Runner
Beating a game once is not enough.

You long to take advantage of in-game mechanics to break the sequence of the game and access areas you're not supposed to be in yet. You're looking to optimize and conquer each room to achieve the fastest clear time.
Designed for Speed Runners
Designed from the ground up for speed running.

The movement mechanics in the game were built to feel fun and fluid, as well as offer the player who has knowledge of the mechanics to sequence break from the intended route.
Become the World's Greatest
Global leaderboards on clearing the boss in the demo for in-game viewing of global standings.
Customize Game Mechanics
The customizer tool allows you to modify your game experience and change abilities to help you progress through the game faster in your own style.
Practice with Tool Assist Mode
Tool Assist Mode which allows you to practice the game at .5x, .75x, and 1x game speed.

Each path from room entry to exit records your score and uploads that route to the global leaderboards. The world record for the route which you took is viewable on leaving a room.
Hardcore
You find enjoyment in frustration.

You long for the pressure of being near achieving a seemingly impossible goal where one small mistake will cause you to lose all your progress.

You're looking for a game that will force you to master all the hidden mechanics and execute them flawlessly for a legendary victory.
Different Game Routes
Select your difficulty through your in game choices

Alternate game path which is accessible from the beginning of the game through knowledge gating which makes the game much more challenging.
Test Your Mastery of Skills
“Secret Final Boss” which requires the player to demonstrate technical mastery over all the techniques found in the game.
