Designed for all types of players
Casual
You enjoy playing games for the story and the experience. You're looking for a game that introduces new mechanics slowly and does not require precise technical input to complete the story.
Kingdom of Light gradually and safely introduces players to new mechanics and enemies before ramping up the difficulty.
Kingdom of Light rewards exploration with lowering the difficulty of the game. If the player gets stuck moving in one direction there’s many other paths the player can explore.

Then they can return to the previously too difficult area powered up and clear it easier with more health, greater damage, or new movement techniques.

Exploration and killing enemies rewards you with money which can be used to purchase movement techniques at the town which will make moving around more fun and dodging enemies or advancing easier.
Kingdom of Light offers multiple paths to the same goal, but guides the player with an invisible hand preventing backtracking to too many areas that the player should not have access to in order to reduce frustration.
The in game map hints to important locations and keeps track of where the player has been so in subsequent play sessions they can pick up where the left off
Designed from the ground up for speed running.

The movement mechanics in the game were built to feel fun and fluid, as well as offer the player who has knowledge of the mechanics to sequence break from the intended route.
Global leaderboards on clearing the boss in the demo for in-game viewing of global standings.
The Customizer tool allows you to modify your game experience and change abilities to help you progress through the game quicker in your own style.
Tool Assist Mode which allows you to practice the game at .5x, .75x, and 1x game speed.

Each path from room entry to exit records your score and uploads that route to the global leaderboards. The world record for the route which you took is viewable on leaving a room.
Speed Runner
Beating a game once is not enough. You long to take advantage of in-game mechanics to break the sequence of the game and access areas you're not supposed to be in yet. You're looking to optimize and conquer each room to achieve the fastest clear time.
Hardcore
You find enjoyment in frustration. You long for the pressure of being near achieving a seemingly impossible goal and one small mistake can cause you to lose all your progress. You're looking for a game that will force you to master all the hidden mechanics and execute them flawlessly for a legendary victory.
Alternate game path which is accessible from the beginning of the game through knowledge gating which makes the game much more challenging.
“Secret Final Boss” which requires the player to demonstrate technical mastery over all the techniques found in the game.
